*August 2023 – Present*

### Project Overview
Developed a sophisticated decision support system for the monitoring of the Nile River Reservoir Dams (NRRD).

### Technologies Used
- Multi-spectral Satellite Analytics
- Artificial Intelligence (Deep Learning for feature extraction)
- Change Detection Algorithms

### Key Outcomes
- The system continuously assesses and reports on various features and status of the dams.
- Enables highly effective, data-driven management and rapid response capabilities.
