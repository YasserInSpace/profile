**Collaboration with PN-RRC and ONPC under funding initiative by IOM**
*August 2025 – January 2026*

### Project Overview
Contributed to the development and delivery of a web-based platform for multi-hazard risk mapping and disaster impact assessment in the Bounkani and Tchologo regions of northeastern Côte d’Ivoire. 

### Technologies Used
- Optical and SAR Imagery Processing
- DEMs & Climate Data Analysis
- Web GIS & Python (Geopandas, Rasterio)

### Key Outcomes
- Processed multi-source datasets to generate hazard, exposure, vulnerability, and risk layers.
- Developed workflows supporting flood and multi-hazard monitoring.
- Facilitated capacity building for national agencies through technical documentation and training, ensuring sustainable system deployment.
