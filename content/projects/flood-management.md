**Collaboration with Agence Sénégalaise d'Études Spatiales (ASES) under funding initiative by Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (METI)**
*January 2025 – January 2026*

Contributed to the design and delivery of a web-based flood monitoring and forecasting application that provides near–real-time situational awareness for riverine flooding. This involved processing and analyzing multi-source Earth-observation data, including optical imagery, SAR, DEMs, and hydrological datasets, to generate flood extent, risk, and vulnerability layers integrated into the platform.

Developed workflows supporting flood monitoring, forecasting, and impact assessment for events along the Senegal and Casamance River basins. Additionally, contributed to the creation of technical documentation, analytical pipelines, and training materials to facilitate system deployment and build stakeholder capacity.
